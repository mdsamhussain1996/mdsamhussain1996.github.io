About Me

I am a mathematician and researcher specialising in fractional-order dynamical systems, quaternion-valued neural networks, and mathematical control theory. I completed my PhD at IIT Mandi (CGPA: 9.67) under the supervision of Prof. Muslim Malik, with a thesis on the synchronisation of fractional-order quaternion-valued neural networks.

My research develops rigorous theoretical frameworks for the analysis and control of complex dynamical systems — from finite-time synchronisation using sliding mode control to the controllability of multi-term fractional impulsive systems with φ-Caputo and Prabhakar derivatives. My work has been published in leading journals including Fractional Calculus and Applied Analysis, Physica Scripta, Chinese Journal of Physics, and The European Physical Journal Plus.

Currently, I serve as an Assistant Professor at Newton School of Technology, Ajinkya DY Patil University, Pune. I bring extensive teaching experience spanning Linear Algebra, Engineering Mathematics, Data Science, and Complex Analysis from my time at IIT Mandi, as well as prior academic support work on platforms such as Chegg and Course Hero.

Research Themes

R · 01
∂
Fractional-Order Dynamics

Prabhakar, φ-Caputo, and Mittag-Leffler kernel frameworks for modelling systems with memory. Controllability and stability of multi-term impulsive fractional systems.

R · 02
ℍ
Quaternion-Valued Neural Networks

Finite-time, projective, quasi-projective, and adaptive synchronisation of fractional quaternion-valued neural networks using sliding mode control and Lyapunov methods.

R · 03
⟳
Synchronisation Theory

Synchronisation of nonidentical, uncertain, and inertial neural networks across fractional orders in the quaternion field — with and without sliding mode control.

R · 04
⊛
Mathematical Control Theory

Controllability analysis of Prabhakar and Langevin dynamical systems with non-instantaneous impulses. Extensions to multi-agent and switched system architectures.

R · 05
⌁
Nonlinear Dynamical Systems

Stability and qualitative analysis of nonlinear ordinary and functional differential equations, including delay and differential-algebraic systems.

R · 06
𝛹
Neural Network Modeling

Competitive, inertial, and recurrent neural network architectures with fractional-order dynamics, uncertainty, and inconsistent orders in the quaternion field.
